# Tabula — Support

Tabula is a small, calm iOS app for logging your daily drinks. If something is broken, missing, or confusing, get in touch — every message is read by the developer.

## Get help

Email **developer@yearncreative.com**

Helpful things to include in your message:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- A screenshot if relevant

We aim to reply within 2 business days.

## Frequently asked

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device, and (if iCloud is enabled in your iOS settings) in your own private iCloud (CloudKit). See the [privacy policy](privacy.md). There are no accounts and no servers under our control.

**My iCloud sync isn't working.**
Make sure iCloud Drive is on in iOS Settings → [your Apple ID] → iCloud, that Tabula is enabled there, and that you're signed in with the same Apple ID across devices. Initial sync can take a few minutes the first time.

**I deleted the app — can I get my data back?**
Yes, if iCloud sync was enabled. Reinstalling Tabula and signing in with the same Apple ID will restore your history. Otherwise, data lives only on the device.

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. Settings → Export will share a prepared summary (including your day-by-day counts) that you can send to ChatGPT, Claude, or save anywhere.

**Does Tabula work with Apple Health?**
Yes. Enable it in Settings → Apple Health to import existing alcohol entries and keep Health in sync going forward.

## Reporting a bug or requesting a feature

Email is fine, but if you'd like to track it publicly you can open an issue at <https://github.com/yearncreative/legal/issues> (this repo is shared with our privacy policy — issues are still the right place for app-level reports until Tabula gets a dedicated issue tracker).
