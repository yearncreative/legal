# Tabula — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-26
**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

Tabula is a personal calendar for tracking standard drinks. It is designed to support moderation and self-awareness. This policy explains exactly what data Tabula handles, where it lives, and what we do (and do not do) with it.

**Short version:** Tabula does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data. There are no accounts, no servers under our control, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. Everything you enter stays on your device and in your own private iCloud account.

Tabula is published by Yearn Creative, an independent developer.
Contact: **developer@yearncreative.com**

## What data Tabula handles

Tabula records the information you enter in the app:

- **Drink counts per day** — the integer number of standard drinks you log.
- **Location flag per day** — whether the entry was "home" or "away" (or both). This is a simple tag, not GPS or any geographic data.
- **Optional secondary counter** — a second tally you can enable (e.g. water, coffee, cannabis) with a label and symbol you choose. Disabled by default.
- **App preferences** — your color theme choice, Trends range selection, and whether Apple Health sync is enabled.

Tabula does **not** collect or store:

- Your name, email, phone number, address, date of birth, or any identity information.
- Account credentials (there are no accounts).
- Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or persistent installation IDs.
- Location data (GPS, coarse location, or any other geographic signal).
- Crash reports, diagnostics, telemetry, or analytics events.
- Contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, or notifications.

## Where your data lives

Your data is stored in two places, both under your control:

1. **On your device**, in Tabula's local SwiftData store.
2. **In your own private iCloud (CloudKit)**, automatically synced across the iPhones and iPads where you are signed into the same Apple ID, if iCloud is enabled in your iOS settings.

We do not operate any server. We never see your data. Apple's iCloud infrastructure is not under our control; we cannot read, query, or access anything stored in your private CloudKit database. Apple's own privacy policy governs how iCloud handles your data (<https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>).

If you disable iCloud for Tabula in your iOS settings, syncing stops and your data lives only on the device.

## Apple Health (optional)

If you enable HealthKit in Tabula, the app can:

- **Read** existing alcohol drink entries from Apple Health to populate Tabula's calendar.
- **Write** the drinks you log in Tabula to Apple Health as alcohol entries.

HealthKit data is stored in Apple Health on your device. It is not sent to us. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Tabula.

If you choose not to enable HealthKit, Tabula does not read or write any Health data.

## What we share

Nothing. We do not sell, rent, share, transmit, or disclose your data to any third party. We do not embed advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social SDKs, or any other third-party tracking.

## Children

Tabula is not directed to children under 17. Because Tabula does not collect any personal information at all, we do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

## Your rights

Because Tabula does not collect data, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on your behalf. You have full local control:

- **Delete a single day** by setting its count to zero.
- **Export all data** to CSV via Tabula → Settings → Export.
- **Delete all data** by deleting the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove Tabula's iCloud data via iOS Settings → [your Apple ID] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Tabula → Delete Data.
